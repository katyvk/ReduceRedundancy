ReduceRedundancy

We've all know we need to help and collaborate - but how if we don't know who's working on what and what they're up to?

Let's increase the efficiency of solution development for Covid19 by connecting people working (or wanting to work) on similar goals!
Find projects working in a space relevant to you so that you can contact and support one another - so that you dont have to reinvent the wheel with your project.

Here's what to do:
(1) Enter your project, or a project you have heard about (,or both), into the form below.
(2) Filter through the list of projects in the sheet to find relevant projects.
(3) Use the comment function to add / change information or to offer support to the project.

Feedback: If you have feedback for us, please input it into the Feedback Form.
